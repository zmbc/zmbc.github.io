About Me

Hi, I'm Zeb. I live in Seattle, Washington. I go to school, make websites, and mess around with data. I've used technologies like Javascript, Ruby, Rails, Ionic, Angular, D3, and Python (from most to least familiar), and I'm always interested in picking up a new one. I'm excited to learn more about data visualization, design, statistics, and computer science. When I'm not on the computer, I enjoy watching and playing basketball, reading, discussing philosophy or political science, and walking through the Arboretum.
