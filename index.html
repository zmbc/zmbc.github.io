About Me

Hi, I'm Zeb. I live in Seattle, Washington. I go to school, make websites, and mess around with data. I've used technologies like Ruby, Rails, Javascript, Ionic, Python, C# and Java (from most to least familiar), but I try to focus on concepts over implementation details. I'm excited to learn more about data visualization, design, statistics, and computer science theory. Outside of technology, I'm a basketball fan and political science fascinates me.
